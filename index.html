View from Alcatraz

Come for the intrigue, stay for the gardens.

The view from the south side of "The Rock" looking towards San Francisco.

The prison and risidual ruins hold interest for some, but if you visit on the best of days, walking around the well maintained gardens is a special treat itself.

In fact, if you're interested in the history of the prison, I suggest checking out this YouTube video and skipping the guided tour itself - more time for the gardens!
